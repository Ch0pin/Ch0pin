## 1. Overview

QR Decoder ("the App") is a simple utility that allows users to scan QR codes using the device camera or decode QR codes from images stored on the device.

We respect your privacy.
This application does **not** collect, store, transmit, or share any personal or sensitive data.

---

## 2. Information We Do NOT Collect

The App does **not** collect or send to any server:

* Personal information
* Contact information
* Location data
* Device identifiers
* Usage analytics
* Images or camera frames
* QR scan results

All processing happens **locally on your device only**.

---

## 3. Camera Access

The App uses the device camera solely for the purpose of scanning QR codes.

* No images are saved.
* No images are uploaded anywhere.
* No camera data leaves the device.

The camera is never used for any other purpose.

---

## 4. Photo Library Access

If you choose to select an image from your gallery:

* The App only reads the selected image to decode the QR code.
* The image is not stored, uploaded, or shared.

---

## 5. Internet Access

The App connects to the internet **only when you tap a decoded URL** to open it in your default browser.

The App itself does not send data to any server.

---

## 6. Third-Party Services

The App uses **Google ML Kit (on-device)** for QR code recognition.
All processing occurs locally; ML Kit does **not** upload data externally.

No third-party SDKs collect data through this App.
