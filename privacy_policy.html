Privacy Policy for QR Decoder

Last updated: November 25, 2025

1. Overview

QR Decoder ("the App") allows users to scan QR codes using the device camera or decode QR codes from images stored on the device. The App does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal or sensitive data.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

The App does not collect or send any of the following data:

  • Personal information
  • Contact information
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics
  • Images or camera frames
  • QR scan results

All QR processing happens locally on your device.

3. Camera Access

The camera is used solely to scan QR codes. No images are saved, uploaded, or transmitted.

4. Photo Library Access

If you choose an image from your gallery, the App only reads the single selected image to decode the QR code. It is not stored or uploaded.

5. Internet Access

The App connects to the internet only if you tap a decoded URL, which opens in your default browser. The App itself does not send data to any server.

6. Third-Party Services

The App uses Google ML Kit (on-device) for QR code recognition. All processing happens locally on your device.
